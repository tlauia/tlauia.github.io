entreniebla is a book of limited edition and published in 2014. It contains diverse explorations of algorithmic visual poetry and codeworks through captures of accidents that were processes in movement, it also includes the source code of each poem, deepening in poetic expression and the potential of programming languages to transform its context and reading.
